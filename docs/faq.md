# Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Profferio?
Profferio is an all-in-one platform that brings together **ticketing**, **time & attendance tracking**, a **dynamic form builder**, **no-code workflows**, **automations**, a **customer portal** and **live reports** — in a single product. Your whole team, in one place.

### Can it replace Jira?
Yes. It covers projects, workflows, conditions/validators, automations and reports comparable to **Jira Service Management** — at a significantly lower cost, and with time tracking and ERGANI integration built in.

### Can I host it on my own servers?
Yes. Profferio runs either in our **secure cloud** or **on-premise** on your own infrastructure, with pricing that depends on the option.

### Does it really connect to ERGANI?
Yes. Time and attendance tracking can be integrated with the Greek **ERGANI** system, so you cut down manual work on schedules and submissions.

### How secure is it?
The platform has passed a **penetration test** and is built around security best practices and **GDPR**. Data stays where you want it — our cloud or your servers.

### How fast can it go live?
Usually within a **few days**. We handle the implementation, the customization to your processes, and the training of your team.

### Is there a contract lock-in?
Pricing is **monthly per user**. We discuss the plan that fits you during the quote — there's no obligation to start.

### How much does it cost?
Pricing is **tailored to your case** — your team size, the plan you choose and your hosting option (cloud or on-premise), plus a one-time setup fee for onboarding and customization. The final quote is shaped after a short needs analysis. [Request a quote](https://www.profferio.com).

### Is my company's data kept separate?
Yes. Each company runs on its **own isolated instance** on a dedicated subdomain (`company.profferio.com`), with separated code and data.

### Is there an AI assistant?
Yes — "Chara", powered by advanced AI, can answer questions about your tickets, team and schedule using live data.

### Is Profferio open source?
No. Profferio is **proprietary, closed-source** software offered as a commercial B2B service. This public repository is a showcase and does not contain the source code.
